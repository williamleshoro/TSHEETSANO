Organization Overview

TSHEETSANO Community Based Organization & Enterprise is a community-driven organization dedicated to transforming lives affected by hunger, poverty, and unemployment through sustainable food security initiatives and organic medicinal plant development.

The organization works closely with communities to empower individuals and families by providing agricultural training, promoting food production, supporting medicinal plant cultivation, and creating economic opportunities that improve livelihoods.

Mission

"To change lives affected by hunger, poverty, and unemployment through food security, organic medicinal plant cultivation, skills development, and sustainable community empowerment programs."

Vision

"To reach people and transform communities by creating opportunities for sustainable livelihoods, improved well-being, and economic growth through agriculture and medicinal plant development."

    Our Core Activities

    Services & Empowerment Programs

    From professional traditional healing wisdom to active food sustainability campaigns, we develop scalable community solutions.

    Traditional Healing

    Holistic physical, mental and emotional diagnostics and remedies integrating traditional African medicine with 20+ years of healing lineage.

    Spiritual Counseling

    Spiritual alignment sessions and ancestral guidance designed to resolve inner blockages, restoring peace, harmony, and systemic balance.

    Indigenous Cultivation

    Pioneering organic cultivation projects of indigenous plants to preserve biodiversity while creating highly potent herbal resources.

    Agricultural Training

    Practical training courses on agro-ecology, compost design, sustainable water harvesting, and high-density community gardening.

    Food Security Programs

    Formulating robust, resilient localized hunger-relief nets by helping neighborhoods establish cooperative gardens and feed projects.

    Community Development

    Strategic planning, cooperative incubation, and collaborative capacity-building to foster self-reliant, highly driven, healthy townships.

    Skills Training

    Workshops covering medicinal herb processing, agricultural management, and commercial-readiness for young local entrepreneurs.

    Job Creation

    Transforming agricultural and natural health projects into enduring hubs of local employment, creating reliable family-level income streams.
